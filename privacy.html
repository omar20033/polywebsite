Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

predictionmarketsacademy ("we", "us") publishes educational content about prediction markets at predictionmarketsacademy.com. This page explains what information we collect when you visit, and how it's used.

Information we collect

  • Usage data. Like most websites, our hosting and analytics providers may automatically log standard technical information — such as your browser type, device, approximate location, referring page, and the pages you visit — to help us understand how the site is used and to keep it running reliably.
  • Information you send us directly. If you email us, we receive whatever you include in that message (such as your email address and the contents of your note). We use this only to respond to you.

We do not require account creation, and we do not ask visitors for financial, wallet, or trading account information to read this site.

Cookies

The site may use minimal cookies or local storage to remember basic preferences (like which filter you last viewed) and, if analytics are enabled, to understand aggregate traffic. We don't use this data to build advertising profiles.

How we use information

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the site
  • To understand which articles are useful, in aggregate
  • To respond to emails sent to us
  • To keep the site secure and prevent abuse

What we don't do

We don't sell your personal information. We don't share your email with third parties for marketing. We don't track your Polymarket wallet or trading activity — any wallet data referenced in our articles comes from public, on-chain sources, not from you.

Third-party services

We may use third-party infrastructure (such as hosting or analytics providers) that process technical data on our behalf under their own privacy policies. Links in our articles may take you to Polymarket or other outside sites, which have their own privacy practices we don't control.

Your choices

You can use browser settings to block or clear cookies at any time. If you've emailed us and would like your message or email address deleted from our records, contact us and we'll take care of it.
