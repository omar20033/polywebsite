Frequently asked questions

Are these trader strategies guaranteed to work?

No. Every breakdown here describes what a specific trader did in a specific market — it's a case study, not a promise. Prediction markets carry real risk of loss, and past results don't predict future ones.

How much capital do I need to start trading strategies like these?

There's no fixed minimum — several breakdowns here start from a few dollars. What matters more than starting size is understanding the mechanics of the specific market before you size up.

What does "fading the headline" mean?

It means betting against the market's reaction to dramatic news, on the view that headline-driven fear or excitement is often overpriced relative to the actual odds of the outcome.

What's the biggest risk with strategy-following?

Copying a strategy without understanding why it worked in that specific market. The same setup can lose money in a different context — these breakdowns explain the reasoning, not just the result.
